Current APE: Regressed APE:
Year - Pick your Year of Regression. This will populate your Regression Percentages.
Regress - Will calculate your Regressed Attribute values based on Current Attribute, Weaknesses/Strengths, and Regression Percentage.
Swap - Places the current Regressed Attribute values in the Current Attribute. Useful for calculating multiple years.
Print - Will give you a popup of your Regressed Attribute values.
Reset - Will reset the entire calculator.

Current Attribute - The value of your attribute prior to this regression calculation.
Wk | Nrm | Str - Weak, Normal, or Strong. Click the corresponding radio if this attribute is a weakness or a strength in your archetype.
Regression Percentage - The percentage (in decimal form) in which each attribute will be regressed.
Regressed Attribute - The value of each Attribute after regression has ruined your player.
